## 📚 Featured Projects

### 🌙 [Sleep Health and Lifestyle Analysis](https://github.com/Little-Ifrit/Sleep-Health-and-Lifestyle)
**Clinical Analytics | Predictive Modeling | Statistical Testing**

Comprehensive analysis of 375+ individuals to identify critical factors influencing sleep disorders and their relationship with lifestyle metrics.

**Key Achievements:**
- 🎯 Identified top 5 sleep disorder risk factors using Random Forest Classification
- 📈 Discovered BMI as strongest predictor (24.3% importance) with p < 0.001 significance
- 🩺 Created actionable clinical profiles for high-risk populations
- 📊 Validated findings using statistical tests (Chi-square, ANOVA)

**Skills Applied:** EDA, Machine Learning, Statistical Analysis, Clinical Insights

**Impact:** Insights can inform lifestyle interventions and clinical decision-making strategies

**Skills Applied:** Data Validation, Feature Engineering, Regression Modeling, Business Problem-Solving
